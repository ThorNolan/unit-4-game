Help Grandpa Take His Pills!

Your grandpa is a tricky one and he likes to mix up his pill bottles every day, and his prescription is always changing. Below grandpa you'll see a number, which is the number you're trying to reach.

Every time you click on one of the four pill bottles, your total pills will go up by some amount (you better be keeping track for grandpa's sake!).

If you give grandpa too many pills, and your number is higher than the number below him, you lose. Better hit that number right on the money and win then!
